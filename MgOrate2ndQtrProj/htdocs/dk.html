Komodo Dragon 2.5

made by: Don Dailey, Larry Kaufman, and Mark Lefler

Official Website & Store of Komodo Dragon

Komodo started on 2007 with its first release on January 2010, and its latest release, the Komodo Dragon 2.5 on September 24 this year. The engine is written on C and C++. The engine is commercial, but its older versions are free. The engine has a playstyle that focuses on evaluation instead of analysis depth.
