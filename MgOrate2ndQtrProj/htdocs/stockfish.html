Official Website for Stockfish

Stockfish is a chess engine created on November 2, 2008, with its latest release (14.1) during October 28 this year. The engine is written on C++ and is an open source project (Stockfish Official Github Page) and can be downloaded in different operating systems including Android and IOS. The engine is ranked high in different chess engine lists, along with its older versions. The engine is used on most chess websites such as chess.com and lichess.org.
